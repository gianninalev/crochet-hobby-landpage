Crochet

Creativity for everyone

Crochet is a type of needlecraft in which you use a hook to create fabric from loops of yarn. Crochet can be used to make everything from blankets, scarves to bags, dresses and sweaters. If you're looking for a fun, creative way to spend your time, crochet may be the perfect activity for you!

Why should I start doing crochet?

  • - You don't need a special talent to do it. Anyone can do crochet: children, young and old people, women, men with and without disabilities.
  • - Not need of a special place to do it. You can do crochet everywhere! On the bus, at a cafe, on the beach. Name it! Everywhere.
  • Benefits. As a creative activity, It impacts in a postive way in your health. It can:
    • Reduce stress and anxiety as relieves depression.
    • Increase your productivity.
    • Lend itself to group social activities.
    • Build your self-esteem by being productive.
